Build Quality

Premium Specifications

No compromise on materials. Every detail specified to deliver lasting quality and comfort that you can see and feel.

🏛️

Foundation & Structure

RCC framed structure with M25 grade concrete, engineered for seismic resistance and long-term durability.

🧱

Walls

Outer walls 9" thick · Inner walls 4½" thick country red brick with cement mortar — solid and thermally efficient.

🖌️

Plastering

Cement mortar with two coats of sponge-finish plastering inside and outside for clean, long-lasting smooth walls.

🚪

Doors

Main door: Teak wood frames with teak paneled shutter & brass fittings. Other doors: Teak frames, flush shutters & SS fittings.

🪟

Windows

UPVC windows with glass, mosquito mesh and safety grills for year-round security and comfort.

⬛

Flooring

Premium 2×4 ft Vitrified tiles throughout all rooms — elegant, durable, and effortlessly maintained.

🍳

Kitchen

Polished black granite platform, SS sink, 2ft digital tile dadoing, municipal & bore water taps, provision for cabinets, exhaust fan & chimney.

🚿

Toilets

EWC with anti-skid ceramic flooring, digital tile dadoing to 8'0". CERA or equivalent sanitary fittings throughout.

🎨

Painting

Internal: 2 coats Asian Paints Premium Emulsion over primer. External: Asian Apex texture finish over primer.

💧

Plumbing

All pipelines in CPVC. CERA or equivalent washbasins in dining area, EWC in all toilets with flush tanks.

⚡

Electrical

Concealed copper wiring with ample light & power points. TV, fridge, geyser & AC provision. Inverter-ready Finolex wiring.

🏢

Extras & Amenities

T-TRANSCO connection, generator backup, lift, electrical meters, car parking, solar fencing and CCTV cameras.
